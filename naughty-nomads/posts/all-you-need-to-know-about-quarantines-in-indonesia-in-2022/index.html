Length of Quarantine Changes Fast üîÄ

COVID is unpredictable, and in my opinion Indonesia responds very fast, which means the length of your quarantine might change when you arrive in Indonesia. But, it doesn‚Äôt necessarily mean you‚Äôd do longer quarantines, responding fast also means they will shorten it when they can.

Length of Our Quarantine

When we were planning to come here, there was a 3 day quarantine, it then got bumped to 7 days and then 10 days because of the omicron variant, and while we were in quarantine it got knocked down to 7 days.

We thought our quarantine was not bad at all, and you most probably will have even a better time than ours because right now in early February 2022 the quarantine length has been shortened to only 5 days.

PCR Test Before Quarantine Ends

You will have a PCR test on the second last morning of your quarantine, including both nasal and throat swab.

Documents You Will Receive

If your result comes back negative you will be free to go the next morning, with the negative test report and a clearance letter in hand.

What Happens If You Test Positive

But if you test positive you will be taken to another government assigned quarantine facility where you will do more quarantine time, and the same goes for if you test positive at the airport before your quarantine.

7 days quarantine is not really 7 days but actually 5 full days. We arrived Tuesday night , then spent Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday and in our room.
